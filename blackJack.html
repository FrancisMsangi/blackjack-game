Blackjack Game

How to Play

Welcome to Blackjack! The goal of the game is to beat the dealer by having a hand value closer to 21 without going over.

  • Click Start Game to begin.
  • Click Deal Card to receive cards.
  • Click Stop to end your turn and let the dealer play.
  • If your hand exceeds 21, you lose (bust).
  • The dealer must draw cards until their score is at least 21 or they beat your score.
